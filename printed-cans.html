About the print

UV printed direct onto the front face of the can. Not a sticker. Not a shrink wrap. The rest of the can stays crystal-clear so customers see the drink from every angle.

Full-wrap printing is available on bulk runs. Get in touch for specifics.

Best for

  • Iced coffee and cold brew
  • Juices, smoothies, kombucha
  • Specialty drinks and cocktails
  • Branded takeaway for cafes
  • Events and product launches
  • Retail-ready packaged beverages
⚙️ Seals on any standard PET machine. Works with CanGo, iPAK, Rosa and every standard seamer. Our cans use industry-standard sizing.
