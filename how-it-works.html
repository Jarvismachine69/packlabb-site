How it works

Artwork to dispatched packaging in four steps.

No sales calls. No setup fees. No mystery on timing. Here is exactly what happens from the moment you order to the moment your boxes show up.

01

Upload your artwork

Send us AI, PDF, PNG or JPG. Print-ready ideal, but not required. Phone photo of a napkin sketch works. Our studio picks it up and tells you what we can do with it.

Takes you
5 min
02

Free digital proof

We mock your artwork on the real can or cup and send it back. You approve, request changes, or cancel. Unlimited revisions until you are happy. Book a visit to see it on the actual machine if you want.

We turn around
≤ 24h
03

Printed in Melbourne

You approve. Payment is taken. We print. UV direct onto the packaging, 6,000 units per hour at full tilt. No plate fees. No MOQ. Order one, order ten thousand, same machine, same quality.

We print in
1 to 2 days
04

Dispatched Australia-wide

Packed, shipped express. Free over $200. Melbourne pickup same day. Interstate 1 to 2 days on top. Any print defect, we reprint. Full stop.

In your hands
2 to 3 days

Repeat customers get the fast lane

Your artwork, stock and specs are saved. One click puts your next run into production. Most cafes reorder weekly.
