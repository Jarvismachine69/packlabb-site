The Process

Simple. Fast.
Done in days.

From your artwork to your hands in 2-3 business days. Here's exactly how it works.

01
Place your order
Choose your size, quantity and type

Pick 330ml or 500ml, printed or unprinted, and how many you need - from 1 sample to 10,000+. Price updates instantly based on your quantity.

Not sure what size or quantity? Start with a sample. Order 1 can for $15.00, get it in 2-3 days, and make your decision from there. We'll take the \.50 off your next order when you place a full order of 200+.

02
Upload your artwork
Send us your logo or design file

Upload your artwork at checkout or after ordering - we'll email you before anything is printed. We accept AI, PDF and high-resolution PNG files.

Our team reviews every file before it goes to print. If there's a resolution issue, a colour problem, or anything that might affect the print quality, we'll tell you before it's a problem. You'll never be surprised by the result.

03
Approve your proof
See it before we print a single can

We send a free digital proof showing exactly how your logo will look on the can. You approve it - then and only then do we print.

This step protects you completely. If you're not happy with the proof, we'll fix it. No can gets printed without your sign-off. Most proofs are turned around within a few hours of receiving your artwork.

04
Printed in Australia
Your cans are printed in our Australian facility

This is why we're fast. While other suppliers wait 2-6 weeks for cans to arrive from overseas, we print in Australia. Once your proof is approved, your order goes straight to print.

Your logo is printed directly on the front face of the can - not a full wrap. Clean, minimal and premium. The rest of the can remains clear so your drink is visible inside.

05
Delivered to your door
In your hands within days

Orders are dispatched the same day if placed before 2pm. Most customers receive their cans within 2-3 business days of placing the order.

Free express shipping on orders over $100. We ship Australia-wide, including Western Australia - see the WA note in the FAQ below.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does it actually take?
+
We print in Australia, not overseas. Once your artwork is approved, your order is dispatched within 1-2 business days. Order before 2pm for same-day dispatch. Standard delivery is then 1-2 days. Most customers have their cans within 2-3 business days total.
How does the sample credit work?
+
Order 1 sample can for $15.00 and see the quality for yourself. When you place a follow-up order of 200 cans or more, we automatically take the $15.00 off that order — no refund process, no requests, nothing to chase up. It just comes off the total at checkout. Simple.
What file format do I need for artwork?
+
We accept AI (Adobe Illustrator), PDF, and high-resolution PNG files. Our team reviews every artwork file before it goes to print and will contact you if anything needs adjusting. You'll always see a free digital proof before we print anything.
Are the cans food-safe?
+
Yes. All our cans are food-grade, BPA-free PET. Suitable for cold beverages including iced coffees, juices, cold brew, kombucha, smoothies and more. The cans form an airtight seal when used with a compatible sealing machine.
Are your cans compatible with other sealing machines?
+
Yes. Our PET cans are compatible with all major sealing machines including CanGo, iPAk, Rosa and our own Packlabb machine. If you already have a machine, our cans will work with it.
I'm in Western Australia - can I order?
+
Yes, we ship to WA. However, under Western Australia's Plan for Plastics (2025), PET cans cannot be used for beverages filled and sealed on-site (e.g. a café sealing an iced latte to order). They can be used for pre-packaged beverages filled off-site. WA customers are responsible for ensuring use complies with state regulations. Visit plasticsbanwa.com.au for full details.
Can I order unprinted cans?
+
Absolutely. We sell both custom printed and unprinted clear cans. Unprinted cans are 5% cheaper and are popular with businesses who want to use their own labels, or just need a clean sealed takeaway container.
What if I don't like my proof?
+
We'll fix it. Nothing is printed without your approval. If the proof doesn't look right, let us know what needs changing and we'll revise it. We don't run a single can until you're happy with what you see.
